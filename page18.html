Physiological flows

MOEBIUS models physiological flows in complex conduits reconstructed from 3-D or 2-D medical imaging enables the characterization of both blood streams in cardiovascular networks and airflow in nasal & pulmonary air-paths. MOEBIUS also includes advanced rheology models, fluid-structure capabilities to include the effect of deformations and particles or cells transport.
This easy-to-use and fast technology pushes the envelope of non-invasive diagnostics tools and helps preventing diseases
