LEXMA Leadership Team

Over the past 25 years, Simone Melchionna held various positions as researcher and instructor in leading academic institutions, such as Harvard, Cambridge University, Ecole Polytechnique Lausanne. Simone Melchionna worked for 5 years as a consultant for the industry to lead R&D projects in cardiovascular diagnostics, medical imaging, and various biomedical applications.

Simone is the author of hundreds of peer-reviewed scientific publications and his activities have profoundly impacted the understanding and the use of computational sciences for biology and physiology applications. Mastering multiple aspects of advanced computational approaches, Simone has a profound experience in supercomputing that led him to receiving prestigious Gordon Bell and Nvidia awards.

Franck Pérot started his career in 2001 as a research engineer at the Automotive Research and Innovation Division of PSA Peugeot Citroën, France. Franck joined Exa Corporation, USA in 2007 where he occupied various roles in application management, customer project support, pre-sales, product management and strategy. Franck has a precise view of the practical impact of computational approaches on the development and R&D processes and a clear vision on the way to develop and deploy the best possible solutions.

Franck holds a Ph.D in computational aeroacoustics from Ecole Centrale de Lyon, France and M.Sc. in mechanical engineering from Ecole Centrale de Marseille, France.
